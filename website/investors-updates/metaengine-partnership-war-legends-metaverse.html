War Legends is built on top of blockchain technology. The game aims to become a unified war zone world that features additional functionalities through LAND ownership. Alongside this, it features conquest, an introduction to strategy elements seen throughout strategy games, and other engaging developments that will entice players to utilize actual strategy and achieve victory.
‍


Besides implementing Play-to-Earn (P2E) mechanics, War Legends also has its own native cryptocurrency in the form of the $WAR token and it utilizes NFTs. Non-fungible token (NFT) tanks are featured in a large variety. Each of the NFTs in the game is a Combat Tank based on actual historical vehicles utilized throughout each side of the war. Each NFT features different statistics which can make a genuine difference on the battlefield and establish the basis of each player’s role in battle. By utilizing NFT tanks, players can shape their destiny with a slow but powerful Gunner-class tank such as a Tiger I or zip across the battlefield with a Cruiser T-34 which is fast but has less power in terms of the damage it brings to the battlefield. Players can also utilize a Sherman Firefly, and this level of customization and freedom is available to each player.

Alongside having LAND NFTs and TANK NFTs, players are also allowed to get Heroes NFTs which play a fundamental role in the game. These are designed by the well-known crypto artist ‘Gehena’. The $WAR Token is the native currency utilized within the game. This cryptocurrency allows token holders to play, invest and also be part of the game. You can earn $WAR tokens through the process of winning battles and utilize them to :

  • Purchase Game Items
  • Receive Staking Rewards
  • Play-to-Earn (P2E) Mechanics
  • Governance Utility

When it comes to the Blockchain aspect of the War Legends game, the game will be utilizing an Ethereum (ETH) and Polygon (MATIC) bridge technology due to its popularity and low cost in terms of fees.

War Legends Release, Staking & NFT Airdrop, Public Token Sale, Exchange Listing, NFT Tank Card Sale, and NFT Ability Card Sale is targeted for Q3 2022 whereas Ships Alpha, Jet Fighters Alpha, War Legends Metaverse, Tournaments, and Expand Content will go live by 2023.

About War Legends

War Legends is a military-themed Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game developed on top of blockchain technology that functions as its own metaverse and has non-fungible tokens (NFTs) alongside Play-to-Earn (P2E) elements.

War Legends is built by a team of experienced gaming and blockchain executives (Playchain Pte. Ltd in Singapore) and is pioneering the Gaming industry by adopting the latest trends and technologies such as yield-generating NFT assets and a growing player-owned economy with the potential to attract millions of gamers.
